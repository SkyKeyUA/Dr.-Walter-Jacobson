Walter E. Jacobson, M.D.

Dr. Jacobson received his Bachelor of Arts degree from Cornell University, his Medical Degree from the Medical College of Wisconsin, and his Psychiatric Training from the UCLA/San Fernando Valley Psychiatry Residency Program.

Dr. Jacobson is a Board-Certified Psychiatrist and has been in private practice in the Los Angeles area since 1999, providing cognitive tools and spiritual techniques to thousands of people which have consistently improved the quality of their lives and maximized their potential for happiness, well-being and material success in all aspects of their lives.
